Available Formats

Get our activities as JSON or Atom.

Pagination & Save Points

Add the page parameter to request the first/second/third/.. 30 activities.

http://asl.hickerspace.org/asl.json?page=2
http://asl.hickerspace.org/asl.atom?twitter.tweet&page=2

If you want only new activities added since your last request, provide the last_id parameter. last_id should be the last activity you are aware of.

http://asl.hickerspace.org/asl.json?last_id=170
http://asl.hickerspace.org/asl.atom?mailing-list&last_id=188

Filter

Add (multiple) parameter(s) in this syntax: service.type[.account] (brackets mark optional parts).

http://asl.hickerspace.org/asl.json?youtube.comment
http://asl.hickerspace.org/asl.json?youtube.comment&youtube.video
http://asl.hickerspace.org/asl.atom?soup.notification&soup.post

Deselect service-type-combinations by putting a minus (-) in front of them.

http://asl.hickerspace.org/asl.json?-youtube.comment

Use asterisks (*) as type/accounts wildcards.

http://asl.hickerspace.org/asl.json?github.*.hickerspace
http://asl.hickerspace.org/asl.json?-twitter.*.hickernews
